Biograph

I received my B.E. degree from Nankai University, Tianjin, China in 2016. I am currently a third-year Ph.D student of Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Shiguang Shan. I also collaborate with Dr. Hu Han closely. My researches mainly focus on affective computing, including remote heart rate estimation, facial action units detection, gaze estimation, etc. I am also interested in basic theories of machine learning and their applications in computer version.
